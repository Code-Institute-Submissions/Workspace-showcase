Showcase your current or preferred remote workspace

The massive disruption caused by Covid-19 caused many of us to adopt new workspace options since we were forced to work-from-home. Soon this will pass, and things will go back to normal. Well, at least there would be a new normal. Remote workers and workspaces could become part of the new normal, so you should start thinking about your ideal workspace.

The ideal workspace differs from one person to the next and can range from a cozy little work-from-bed set up to a full-blown home office built as a tribute to your old office space. The end goal with any workspace should be to improve your productivity while keeping you happy. This app's idea is to showcase your 'away-from-office' workspace and get inspired by other workspaces submitted by people from around the world. You can rate your happiness index for your current workspace and share what makes it special for you.

You can view the different workspace submissions by clicking on the 'Show workspaces' button below.
